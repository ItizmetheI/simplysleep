How to Test a Mattress In Store

  • Lie in your normal sleep position for several minutes.
  • Check whether your shoulders, hips, and lower back feel cushioned without sagging.
  • Sit and lie near the edge to test perimeter stability.
  • Roll from side to back to see how responsive the surface feels.
  • Ask about trial period, return fees, warranty terms, delivery, and haul-away options.

These shopper checks mirror the pressure relief, alignment, edge support, response, and setup categories used by Simply Rest Lab.
